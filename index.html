Point of Pines: Possibly the best little neighborhood in the Boston area.

Located just outside of the City of Boston, the Point of Pines neighborhood of Revere features 200 acres of private beachfront - for the exclusive use of its residents and their guests. The neighborhood is a gateway to communities and additional beaches further along the north shore, such as Nahant, Swampscott, and Marblehead.

Before it became a residential neighborhood in the early 20th century, the Point of Pines served as the weekend getaway of the Beacon Hill elite, a short ferry or trolly ride from the city. The area featured a large hotel, with hundreds of acres of picnic grounds, sports facilities, and of course, the beach.
